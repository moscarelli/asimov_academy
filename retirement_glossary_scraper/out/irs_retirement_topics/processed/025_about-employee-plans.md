# About Employee Plans
## Overview of the IRS Office of Employee Plans and Retirement Plans Oversight

The Employee Plans office is responsible for enforcing the federal tax laws governing retirement plans. We oversee and enforce compliance with these laws to protect employees, employers, and the public.

### What is the Employee Plans Office?

The Employee Plans office is part of the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) and works closely with the Department of Labor (DOL), the Pension Benefit Guaranty Corporation (PBGC), and other federal agencies to ensure compliance with retirement plan laws.

### Outreach and Educational Services

We provide outreach and educational services to help employers, employees, and other stakeholders understand their rights and responsibilities under federal retirement plan laws. These services include:

*   Requesting a speaker for a conference or event
*   Registering for a free phone forum or webinar
*   Viewing prior Tax Forum presentations
*   Reporting an abusive transaction involving a retirement plan
*   Sharing information about an abusive tax shelter or transaction (anonymously)
