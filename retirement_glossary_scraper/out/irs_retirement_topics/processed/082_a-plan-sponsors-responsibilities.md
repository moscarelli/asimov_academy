# A Plan Sponsor's Responsibilities
## Overview

As a plan sponsor, you are responsible for maintaining compliance with the Internal Revenue Code and administering your employee retirement plan according to its terms.

### Understanding Your Responsibilities

*   Review your plan document annually to ensure it remains compliant with all requirements.
*   Understand the options listed in your adoption agreement and know what they cover.
*   Know your fiduciary responsibilities and correct the plan if it becomes non-compliant.

## Maintaining Compliance

*   File required forms and documents with the IRS or Department of Labor.
*   Determine if testing is required, and run it timely.
*   Maintain accurate records for participant accounts.
*   Invest plan funds and review any associated fees.
*   Learn about your fiduciary responsibilities.

### Working with Your Provider

*   Communicate with your pre-approved plan provider to learn about fees and stay informed about changes to the plan's terms.
*   Promptly sign plan amendments sent by the provider.
*   Inform the provider of changes to your business, employees, or compensation.

## Staying Informed

*   Review your service provider's reports, such as the allocation report for possible contribution errors.
*   Ensure participants have timely started required minimum distributions.
