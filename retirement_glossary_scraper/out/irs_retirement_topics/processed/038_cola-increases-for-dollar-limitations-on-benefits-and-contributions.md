# COLA Increases for Dollar Limitations on Benefits and Contributions

The tax law places limits on the dollar amount of contributions to retirement plans and IRAs, as well as the amount of benefits under a pension plan. [IRC Section 415](https://www.irs.gov/individuals-retirees/information-for-individuals) requires these limits to be adjusted annually for cost-of-living increases.

## Limits by Plan Type

### IRA
*   **2025:** $7,000
*   **2024:** $7,000
*   **2023:** $6,500
*   **2022:** $6,000
*   **IRA Catch-up Contributions:** $1,000

### 401(k), 403(b), Profit-Sharing Plans, etc.
*   **2025:** Annual compensation: $350,000, Elective deferrals: $23,500, Defined contribution limits: $70,000
*   **2024:** Annual compensation: $345,000, Elective deferrals: $23,000, Defined contribution limits: $69,000
*   **2023:** Annual compensation: $330,000, Elective deferrals: $22,500, Defined contribution limits: $66,000
*   **2022:** Annual compensation: $305,000, Elective deferrals: $20,500, Defined contribution limits: $61,000

### SIMPLE Plans
*   **2025:** Maximum contributions: $16,500, Catch-up contributions: $3,500
*   **2024:** Maximum contributions: $16,000, Catch-up contributions: $3,500
*   **2023:** Maximum contributions: $15,500, Catch-up contributions: $3,500
*   **2022:** Maximum contributions: $14,000, Catch-up contributions: $3,000

### 457(b) Defined Benefit Plans
*   **2025:** Elective deferrals: $23,500
*   **2024:** Elective deferrals: $23,000
*   **2023:** Elective deferrals: $22,500
*   **2022:** Elective deferrals: $20,500

### Other Plans (e.g., ESOPs)
*   **2025:** HCE threshold: $160,000, Defined benefit limits: $280,000
*   **2024:** HCE threshold: $155,000, Defined benefit limits: $275,000
*   **2023:** HCE threshold: $150,000, Defined benefit limits: $265,000
*   **2022:** HCE threshold: $135,000, Defined benefit limits: $245,000

### Key Employee Plans (e.g., 457 Plan)
*   **2025:** Elective deferrals: $23,500
*   **2024:** Elective deferrals: $23,000
*   **2023:** Elective deferrals: $22,500
*   **2022:** Elective deferrals: $20,500

### Control Employee Plans (e.g., 457 Plan)
*   **2025:** Elective deferrals: $23,500
*   **2024:** Elective deferrals: $23,000
*   **2023:** Elective deferrals: $22,500
*   **2022:** Elective deferrals: $20,500

### Taxable Wage Base
*   **2025:** $176,100
*   **2024:** $168,600
*   **2023:** $160,200
*   **2022:** $147,000
