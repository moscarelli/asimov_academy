# Conferences and Educational Services

## Overview

The Internal Revenue Service (IRS) offers conferences and educational services to help individuals, businesses, and other organizations understand tax laws and regulations.

## Conferences

* IRS Nationwide Tax Forums: Presentations and slides from employee plans presentations.
* Requesting educational services from Tax-Exempt & Government Entities: Request an experienced IRS agent for speeches or panel discussions.

## Educational Services

* Request an experienced IRS agent for speeches or panel discussions.
* Operate a retirement plan: Information on required minimum distributions, published guidance, and forms.
* Forms and publications: List of available forms and publications.
* IRS Nationwide Tax Forum presentations - Retirement plans topics
* Taxpayer Bill of Rights
* Taxpayer Advocate Service

## Help Resources

* Find a Local Office: Information on local offices and their hours of operation.
* Know Your Rights: Overview of taxpayer rights.
* Resolve an Issue: Information on resolving issues with the IRS.
