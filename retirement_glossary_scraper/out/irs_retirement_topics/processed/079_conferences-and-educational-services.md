# Conferences and Educational Services

## Overview

Conferences and educational services are designed to help you learn about the tax laws and regulations, as well as other topics related to taxes.

### Types of Conferences and Educational Services

The IRS offers various conferences and educational services for individuals, businesses, charities, and nonprofit organizations. Some of these include:

*   **Taxpayer Advocate Service**: This service provides assistance with issues related to individual taxpayer rights.
*   **Office of the Taxpayer Advocate**: This office advocates on behalf of taxpayers and works to resolve disputes between taxpayers and the IRS.
*   **National Tax Forums**: These forums provide opportunities for tax professionals and individuals to learn about topics such as retirement plans, IRAs, and more.
*   **Training and Education**: The IRS offers various training programs for tax professionals, including courses on tax law, compliance, and more.

### Requesting Educational Services

If you are interested in requesting educational services from the IRS, you can do so by:

*   Contacting your local IRS office
*   Calling the IRS Information Number (1-800-829-1040)
*   Submitting a request through the IRS website

The IRS will work with you to provide educational services that meet your needs.

### Types of Educational Services

Some examples of educational services offered by the IRS include:

*   **Tax Law Updates**: The IRS provides updates on tax law changes and other relevant information.
*     **Compliance Assistance**: The IRS offers assistance with compliance issues, such as filing requirements and deadlines.
*   **Taxpayer Advocacy**: The IRS advocates on behalf of taxpayers in disputes with the agency.

These educational services are designed to help individuals and businesses understand their tax obligations and navigate the complexities of tax law.
