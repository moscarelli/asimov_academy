# Required Minimum Distributions
## Introduction

The IRS provides guidance on required minimum distributions (RMDs) from Individual Retirement Accounts (IRAs). RMDs are mandatory distributions from IRAs, including SEP IRAs and SIMPLE IRAS.

## Types of RMDs

*   **Required Minimum Distribution Worksheet**: Use this worksheet if your spouse is the sole beneficiary of your IRA and is more than 10 years younger than you.
*   **For Everyone Else (Use If Worksheet Above Does Not Apply)**: Use this worksheet for all other situations.
