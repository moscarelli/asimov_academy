# Retirement Topics - Catch-up Contributions

If you're 50 or older, you can make catch-up contributions to your retirement plan.

## Annual Catch-up Contribution Limits

*   For tax year 2023 and 2024: $7,500
*   For tax years 2021-2020 and 2015-2019: $6,500
*   For tax year 2022: $6,000
*   For tax years 2019-2021: $19,000

## Eligible Plans

Catch-up contributions may be permitted in the following plans:

*   401(k) (other than a SIMPLE 401(k))
*   403(b)
*   SARSEP
*   Governmental plans
*   457(b)

## Elective Deferrals

Elective deferrals are not treated as catch-up contributions until they exceed $23,000 or the ADP test limit of section 401(k)(3) or the plan limit.

## Catch-up Contribution Methods

Catch-up contributions must be made before the end of the plan year and via elective deferrals.
