# Form 8955-SSA Resources

## Introduction

The Internal Revenue Service (IRS) provides various resources to help individuals and organizations complete and submit Form 8955-SSA. This form is used for reporting and paying the Social Security and Medicare taxes withheld on certain types of retirement income.

### Understanding Form 8955-SSA

*   **Purpose**: The purpose of Form 8955-SSA is to report and pay the Social Security and Medicare taxes withheld on certain types of retirement income.
*   **Who needs to file**: This form is required for individuals and organizations that receive retirement income, such as annuities or pensions.

### Filing Instructions

*   **E-file**: The IRS offers an electronic filing option through its FIRE (Filing Information Returns Electronically) system.
*   **Paper filing**: Forms can be mailed to the address listed on the form.
*   **Private delivery services**: Private companies that provide delivery services must send forms to the Internal Revenue Service at the address listed.

### Frequently Asked Questions

*   **Form 8955-SSA replacement**: The IRS has replaced Schedule SSA with Form 8955-SSA, effective January 1, 2025.
*   **FIRE (Filing Information Returns Electronically)**: This system provides information required to file Form 8955-SSA.
