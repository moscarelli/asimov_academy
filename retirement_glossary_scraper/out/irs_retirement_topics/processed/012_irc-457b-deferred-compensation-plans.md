# IRC 457(b) Deferred Compensation Plans
## Overview

401(k)-like plans that allow employees to defer income taxation on retirement savings into future years.

### Who can establish a 457(b) plan?

*   State or local governments
*   Tax-exempt organizations under Section 501(c)

### How do 457(b) plans work?

*   Employers or employees through salary reductions contribute up to the $23,000 limit in 2024.
*   Contributions are tax-deferred, and earnings on retirement money are also tax-deferred.

### Advantages of participating in a 457(b) plan

*   Tax advantages for participants
*   Contributions to a 457(b) plan are tax-deferred
*   Earnings on the retirement money are tax-deferred
*   Designated Roth accounts may be included in some plans
