# Retirement Topics 403b Contribution Limits
## Introduction

The IRS provides guidance on retirement plan contribution limits, including those for 403(b) plans.

## 403(b) Contribution Limits

### Limit on Employee Elective Salary Deferrals

*   $23,000 in 2024
*   $22,500 in 2023
*   $20,500 in 2022
*   $19,500 in 2021 and 2020

### Catch-up Contributions for Employees Age 50 or Over

*   $7,500 in 2024 and 2023
*   $6,500 in 2022
*   $6,500 in 2021 and 2020

## Limit on Annual Additions

The limit on annual additions (employer contributions + employee elective salary deferrals) is the lesser of:

*   $69,000 in 2024 ($66,000 for 2023; $61,000 for 2022; $58,000 for 2021)
*   100% of includible compensation for the employee's most recent year of service

## Special Rules

*   Employees with at least 15 years of service can have an elective deferral limit increased by the lesser of:
    *   $3,000
    *   $15,000, reduced by the amount of additional elective deferrals made in prior years
    *   $5,000 times the number of the employee's years of service for the organization, minus the total elective deferrals made for earlier years
*   Employees who qualify for the 15-year rule can have an elective deferral limit as high as $26,000 for 2024 and $25,500 for 2021.

## Correcting Mistakes

If you've incorrectly made a catch-up contribution or exceeded the standard limit, find out how to correct this mistake.
