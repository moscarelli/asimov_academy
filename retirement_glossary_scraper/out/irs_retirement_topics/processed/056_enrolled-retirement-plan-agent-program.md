# Enrolled Retirement Plan Agent Program
The Enrolled Retirement Plan (ERPA) Agent program is designed to help retirement plan agents meet their tax preparation and filing requirements.

## Who Can Become an ERPA Agent?

To become an ERPA agent, you must be a qualified individual with expertise in retirement plans. You can also find information on becoming an annual Filing Season Program participant or an enrolled actuary.

## Requirements

The main requirement for enrollment is that you have knowledge of the tax laws governing retirement plans.

### Changes to the Program

There are regular changes to the program, so it's essential to stay up-to-date with the latest developments. You can find information on ERPA program changes and how to maintain your status in the frequently asked questions section.

## Benefits

By becoming an ERPA agent, you will have access to various resources and benefits, including:

*   Tax record (transcript)
*   Apply for an Employer ID Number (EIN)
*   Amend return
*   Identity Protection PIN (IP PIN)

## Contact Information

For more information or to contact the Enrolled Retirement Plan Agent program, you can find the necessary details on the IRS website.

### Frequently Asked Questions

There are answers available in the frequently asked questions section. You can also find more detailed information about ERPA agents and their roles in tax preparation and filing.
