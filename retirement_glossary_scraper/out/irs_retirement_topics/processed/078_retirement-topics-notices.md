### Retirement Topics - Notices

#### Information for Individuals
### Filing For Individuals

*   **How to File**: Submit your tax return, payment, or any other related documents on time to avoid penalties and interest.
*   **When to File**: File your tax return by the deadline, which is typically April 15th for most individuals.
*   **Where to File**: You can file your tax return electronically through the IRS website, or you can mail a paper return to the address listed in the instructions.

#### Popular Topics

*   **Tax Record (Transcript)**: Obtain a copy of your tax transcript to verify information with the IRS.
*   **Apply for an Employer ID Number (EIN)**: Apply online or by phone to obtain an Employer Identification Number (EIN) from the IRS.
*   **Amend Return**: File Form 1040-X to amend your previously filed tax return.

#### Credits and Deductions

*   **Earned Income Credit (EITC)**: Claim the Earned Income Credit if you meet certain eligibility requirements and file your tax return on time.
*   **Child Tax Credit**: Claim the Child Tax Credit if you have qualifying children under age 17.
