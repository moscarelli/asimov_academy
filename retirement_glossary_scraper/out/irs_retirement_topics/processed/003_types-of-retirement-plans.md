# Types of Retirement Plans
Types of retirement plans available to individuals include:

*   IRAs (Individual Retirement Arrangements)
*   Roth IRAs
*   401(k) plans
*   SIMPLE 401(k) plans
*   403(b) plans
*   SIMPLE IRA plans (Savings Incentive Match Plans for Employees)
*   SEP plans (Simplified Employee Pension)
*   SARSEP plans (Salary Reduction Simplified Employee Pension)
*   Payroll deduction IRAs
*   Profit-sharing plans
*   Defined benefit plans
*   Money purchase plans
*   Employee stock ownership plans (ESOPs)
*   Governmental plans, including 457 plans and multiple employer plans

**Choosing a Retirement Plan**
The IRS offers various resources to help individuals choose a retirement plan that meets their needs. These include:

*   A comprehensive list of available retirement plans
*   Information on the benefits and drawbacks of each plan
*   Guidance on how to enroll in a plan
*   Tools for comparing different plans and providers
