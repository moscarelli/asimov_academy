# Actuarial Tables

## Overview

These actuarial tables require valuation of annuities, life estates, remainders and reversions. Certain exceptions apply.

## Interest Rate

* The section 7520 rate is applied:
	+ 120% of the applicable federal mid-term rate in effect for the month when the valuation date falls.
	+ Rounding to the nearest 2 tenths of a percent.
* Alternative rates may be used for certain charitable transfers.
