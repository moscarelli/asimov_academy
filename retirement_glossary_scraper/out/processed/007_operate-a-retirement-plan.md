# Operate a Retirement Plan

## Introduction

To operate a retirement plan, you must keep it in compliance with the law. This includes reporting information on the plan each year and filing Form 5500.

### Forms and Publications

*   Form 1040: Individual Income Tax Return
*   Form 1040 Instructions: Instructions for Form 1040
*   Form W-9: Request for Taxpayer Identification Number (TIN) and Certification
*   Form 4506-T: Request for Transcript of Tax Return
*   Form W-4: Employee's Withholding Certificate
*   Form 941: Employer's Quarterly Federal Tax Return
*   Form W-2: Wage and Tax Statement
*   Form 9465: Installment Agreement Request

## Types of Retirement Plans

*   IRAs (Individual Retirement Accounts)
*   401(k) plans
*   SIMPLE plans
*   SEP plans

### Small Businesses

If you sponsor a plan, you must keep it in compliance.

#### Operate and Maintain Your Plan

You can fix most errors without contacting us. You must report information on the retirement plan each year. File Form 5500. Apply for a letter if you can apply for a determination or opinion letter.
