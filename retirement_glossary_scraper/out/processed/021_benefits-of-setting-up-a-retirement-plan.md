Benefits of setting up a retirement plan
=====================================

A secure retirement is within reach by choosing the right retirement plan. We'll guide you along the way!

Did you know...

* Retirement can last for 30 years or more?
* You may need up to 80% of your current annual income to retire comfortably?
* The average monthly benefit paid by the Social Security Administration is $1,200?

Why set up a retirement plan?
-----------------------------

A retirement plan has numerous benefits:

### Business Benefits

* Employer contributions are tax-deductible
* Assets in the plan grow tax-free
* Plan options are flexible
* Tax credits and other benefits can help reduce costs

### Employee Benefits

* Employee contributions can reduce current taxable income
* Contributions and investment gains are not taxed until distributed
* Contributions are easy to make through payroll deductions
* Interest accrues over time, allowing small, regular contributions to grow significantly

What are the First Steps?
-------------------------

A good starting point is to contact a tax professional familiar with retirement plans or a financial institution that offers retirement plans. Helpful reading materials and IRS websites can be found at the end of this page.

The Stages of Sponsoring a Retirement Plan
--------------------------------------------

1. **Choosing**: Selecting a plan by considering how much money you'll need in retirement and learning about tax-qualified retirement plans.
2. **Establishing**: Putting your plan in place, including adopting a written plan, arranging a trust, notifying eligible employees, and creating a recordkeeping system.
3. **Operating**: Operating your plan to ensure the assets continue to grow and the tax benefits are preserved, covering eligible employees, making contributions, keeping the plan up-to-date, managing plan assets, providing information, and distributing benefits.
4. **Terminating**: Closing out the plan and notifying the appropriate parties when it no longer suits your business.
