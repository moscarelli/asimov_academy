### Retirement Topics - Notices

#### Information for Individuals
- **Tax record (transcript)**: Apply for a transcript of your tax return
- **Apply for an Employer ID Number (EIN)**: Get your Employer Identification Number (EIN)
- **Amend return**: File Form 1040-X to amend your individual income tax return
- **Identity Protection PIN (IP PIN)**: Protect your identity with an IP PIN
- **Pay**: Choose how you want to pay your taxes

#### Information for Businesses & Self-Employed
- **Standard mileage and other information**: Get the standard mileage rate and other information for business expenses
- **Earned Income Credit (EITC)**: Check if you're eligible for the Earned Income Tax Credit
- **Child Tax Credit**: Claim your child tax credit
- **Clean Energy and Vehicle Credits**: Learn about credits for clean energy and vehicle purchases

#### Forms & Instructions
- **Form 1040**: Individual Income Tax Return
- **Form 1040 Instructions**: Get instructions for Form 1040
- **Request for Transcript of Tax Return (Form 4506-T)**: Request a transcript of your tax return
- **Employee's Withholding Certificate (Form W-4)**: Understand how withholding works on your paycheck
- **Employer's Quarterly Federal Tax Return (Form 941)**: File quarterly federal income tax returns as an employer

#### Retirement Plans
- **Types of retirement plans**: Learn about the different types of retirement plans
- **Required minimum distributions**: Understand the rules for required minimum distributions from a retirement plan
- **Published guidance**: Get published guidance on various topics related to retirement plans
- **Operate a retirement plan**: Learn how to operate your retirement plan and provide necessary information to employees

#### Participant Events
- **Notice when employee starts in plan**: Provide documents to employees before they start working for you, depending on the type of retirement plan
- **Automatic enrollment**: Give notice to employees at least 30 days but no more than 90 days before automatically enrolling them in a retirement plan
- **Individual benefits statement (IBS)**: Furnish individual benefits statements to participants upon their written request or automatically to certain participants who have terminated service

#### Notices of Survivor Annuities
- **QJSA**: Get notice about the Qualified Joint and Surviving Annuity (QJSA) feature in your retirement plan, which provides a lifetime annuity plus a survivor annuity for your spouse if they outlive you
- **QOSA**: Understand the requirements for QOSA notices in plans that contain this feature
