# Employee Stock Ownership Plans (ESOPs)
A qualified defined contribution plan that is a stock bonus plan or a stock bonus/money purchase plan.

## ESOP Requirements
An ESOP must be designed to invest primarily in qualifying employer securities as defined by IRC section 4975(e)(8) and meet certain requirements of the Code and regulations. The IRS and Department of Labor share jurisdiction over some ESOP features.

## Anti-Cutback Relief (2013)
Notice 2013-17 offers relief for certain ESOP amendments that eliminate a distribution option to meet new diversification requirements.

## S Corporation Stock Held by an ESOP - Deductions
An ESOP-owned S corporation cannot deduct retirement plan contributions for ESOP participants.
