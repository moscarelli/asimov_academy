# Retirement Plans
## Introduction

Retirement plans are a type of savings plan designed to help individuals save for their retirement.

## Types of Retirement Plans

*   IRAs (Individual Retirement Arrangements)
    *   Traditional IRAs
    *   Roth IRAs
    *   SIMPLE IRA plans
    *   SEP plans
    *   401(k) plans
*   Life Events
    *   Hardship distributions: Early withdrawals and loans
    *   Rollovers: Move money from one plan to another
    *   Changes in your life

## Plan Administration

*   Correcting plan errors
*   IRS examination (audit) of retirement plans
*   Reporting and disclosure
    *   Determination, opinion and advisory letters
    *   Calculate plan benefits:
        *   Interest rate tables
        *   Actuarial tables
