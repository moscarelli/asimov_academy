# COLA Increases for Dollar Limitations on Benefits and Contributions

## Introduction

The tax law places limits on the dollar amount of contributions to retirement plans and IRAs, as well as the amount of benefits under a pension plan. These limits are adjusted annually for cost-of-living increases.

| --- | --- |
| 2025 | $7,000 |
| 2024 | $7,000 |
| 2023 | $6,500 |
| 2022 | $6,000 |
