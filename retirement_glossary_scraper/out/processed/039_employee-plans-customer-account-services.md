# Employee Plans Customer Account Services

The IRS offers a range of services to help employees and their families with retirement plans.

## Filing for Individuals

### How to File

*   When to file: The deadline for filing depends on the type of tax return.
*   Where to file: You can file online or by mail. You can also use the Electronic Federal Tax Payment System (EFTPS) to make payments.
*   Update your information: If you need to update your address or other information, you can do so on the IRS website.

### Popular Topics

*   **Tax Record (Transcript)**: Obtain a copy of your tax return transcript, which includes your Social Security number and tax information.
*   **Apply for an Employer ID Number (EIN)**: Apply online for an Employer Identification Number (EIN), which is used to file taxes as an employer.
*   **Amend Return**: Amending a tax return is the process of changing or correcting previously filed information.

## Paying and Refunds

### Overview

The IRS offers several options for paying taxes, including:

*   **Bank Account (Direct Pay)**: Pay your taxes directly from your bank account using Electronic Federal Tax Payment System (EFTPS).
*   **Payment Plan (Installment Agreement)**: Set up a payment plan to pay off your tax debt in monthly installments.
*   **Electronic Federal Tax Payment System (EFTPS)**: Make payments online or by phone.

### Where's My Refund?

Check the status of your refund online or by calling 1-800-829-1040.

## Credits and Deductions

### Overview

*   **Earned Income Credit (EITC)**: A tax credit for low-to-moderate-income working individuals.
*   **Child Tax Credit**: A tax credit for families with qualifying children under age 17.
*   **Clean Energy and Vehicle Credits**: Tax credits for purchasing or installing clean energy equipment, such as solar panels.

## Forms

### Overview

The IRS offers several forms to help you file your taxes:

*   **Form 1040**: The individual income tax return form.
*   **Form W-9**: A request for taxpayer identification number (TIN) and certification.
*   **Form 941**: An employer's quarterly federal tax return.

### Popular Forms

*   **Form 1040-X**: Amended individual income tax return.
*   **Form 2848**: Power of attorney form.
