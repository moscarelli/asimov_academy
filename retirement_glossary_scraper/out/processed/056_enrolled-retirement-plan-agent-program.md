# Enrolled Retirement Plan Agent Program

## Overview

The Enrolled Retirement Plan (ERPA) Agent program is a certification program offered by the Internal Revenue Service (IRS). It provides enrolled retirement plan agents with training and education to help them prepare tax returns for employees and employers.

### Filing for Individuals

#### How to File

*   When to file: File your tax return by April 15th of each year.
*   Where to file: File your tax return electronically or by mail using Form 1040.
*   Update your information: If you have changes in your income, address, or other information, update it on the IRS website.

### Your Online Account

#### Tax Withholding Estimator

The tax withholding estimator is a tool that helps you estimate your taxes and how much you should have withheld from your paycheck.

#### Estimated Taxes

Estimated taxes are payments made throughout the year to cover taxes owed when you file your return.

#### Penalties

If you fail to make estimated tax payments, penalties may apply. You can avoid or reduce these penalties by making timely payments.
